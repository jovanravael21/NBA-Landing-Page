ABOUT NBA


"The National Basketball Association (NBA) is the premier professional basketball league in the world, featuring the most talented athletes and iconic teams in the sport. Founded in 1946, the NBA has grown into a global phenomenon with 30 franchises representing cities across the United States."


"With superstars like LeBron James, Stephen Curry, Giannis Antetokounmpo, and Luka Dončić, the NBA delivers high-octane action, unforgettable highlights, and intense rivalries every season. From the Regular Season to the dramatic Playoffs and the race to the NBA Finals, every game showcases elite skill, strategy, and passion."


"The NBA isn’t just a league—it’s a culture. With a massive international fanbase, social media presence, and influence on music, fashion, and entertainment, the NBA stands at the intersection of sport and global pop culture."
